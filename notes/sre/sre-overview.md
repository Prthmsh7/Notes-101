## What this topic covers

SRE revision works best when reliability targets, alert quality, incident response, and capacity planning are tied together.

## Revision checkpoints

- Track user-facing signals, not just infrastructure noise.
- Treat runbooks as part of the system.
- Understand the tradeoff between velocity and reliability.
